How it works

Three steps to a better cold email.

Warmwire lives inside your email client. Fill in a few details about your prospect and it generates a personalised, human-sounding draft — then you review and send it yourself.

1

Add prospect details

Enter the recipient, their company, role, and any personal context you've gathered. Warmwire uses this to make every email feel hand-written.

2

Generate & customise

Warmwire drafts a personalised email using your profile and prospect context. Edit it until it sounds like you — not a bot.

3

Fill compose & send

One click fills your email client's compose window with the recipient, subject, and body. You review and hit send — Warmwire never sends on your behalf.
